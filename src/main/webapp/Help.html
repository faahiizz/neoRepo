Getting Started

Welcome to the PennyTracker! This guide will help you navigate through the main features of the application.

1. Creating an Account

To use the full functionality of the PennyTracker, you need to create an account. Click on the "Sign Up" button on the homepage and fill in the required information. After registration, you can log in using your username and password.

2. Adding Income and Expenses

After logging in, you can start tracking your finances by adding your income and expenses. Navigate to the dashboard and enter your monthly income. Then, you can add expenses by specifying the category and amount. The system will automatically calculate your savings and display the data in charts for easy analysis.

3. Viewing and Managing Data

All your financial data is displayed in the summary section on the dashboard. You can view, edit, or delete entries as needed. Use the filter options to see data for specific months or categories.

4. Using Charts

The PennyTracker provides visual representations of your financial data through various charts. These charts help you understand your spending patterns and make informed decisions.

5. Updating Profile and Settings

You can update your profile information and customize your settings by clicking on the "Profile" and "Settings" buttons in the header. Make sure to save any changes you make.

6. Getting Support

If you encounter any issues or have any questions, you can contact our support team by clicking on the "Contact Us" button in the footer. We are here to help!

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my password?

To reset your password, click on the "Forgot Password" link on the login page and follow the instructions.

Can I export my financial data?

Currently, the Personal Finance Tracker does not support exporting data. However, we are working on adding this feature in future updates.

Is my data secure?

Yes, we take data security seriously. Your information is encrypted and stored securely. Please review our Privacy Policy for more details.
